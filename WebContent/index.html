ABOUT ME

I Want To Travel The World

I really don't know how to define myself because all the experiences on-road as well as off has taught me to think beyond the standard definitions. Although Web Developer by profession, it is my quest for forging humane relationships and curiosity to explore new places that has made me a fervent traveler, by that benchmark YES I am a traveler. Whenever on the go, I like to capture the memories, energies, faces that each destination has to offer and what’s better way of capturing the precious moments other than through pictures. My Camera and Mobile Phone are my permanent companion on all my travels. A bike enthusiast, it’s my preferred choice of travel whenever possible, as nothing is more surreal than riding with breezy wind blowing in your face.

Majid Zz


My love for traveling not just stems from curiosity to explore new places but the journey en-route to various places that really gets me excited because I truly believe that the best stories, best souls and best memories are all out there on the streets just waiting to be picked up. My love for cinema has only whet my appetite for traveling across the world. Through this blog I would share my stories and perspectives on places or events. The blog has travelogues, travel tips, travel videos and plenty of memories captured by my lens. So I leave it to you, explore the blog and decide by yourself actually who I am.
